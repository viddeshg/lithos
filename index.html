What is a reservation?

Reservation secures a priority placement for the delivery of the first limited Neematic series, the first 50 production bikes. Reservation is not a complete pre-order or sale.

What does reservation provide?

Individual priority number (1 to 50) as all the bikes will be numbered and delivered in this sequence. The number of the bike will depend on when the reservation was completed online.

Discount of EUR 200 to the final price (EUR 8000+VAT+ shipment costs) which will be applied in the purchase agreement that NEEMATIC will propose in the beginning of 2018.

Cancellation

Reservation Deposits are placed into escrow and are refundable in full, at any time before the purchase agreement has been signed, for any reason, at your request.

Delivery

The first batch limited delivery is intended, but not guaranteed, in summer 2018. Depositors will receive updates on delivery dates for their area as development, testing, and other milestones are achieved.

Motor

With mid-drive motor concept Neematic minimizes wheel sprung mass which gives excellent off-road performance at any speed.

A permanent-magnet brushless outrunner motor delivers Neematic 15 kW max power, an unmatched capacity.

Battery

Neematic runs on the latest tech Lithium-ion 2.2 kWh battery pack. It delivers up to 100 km range and enables the motor for maximum torque.

Battery is quickly removable and charges fully in 2.5 hours using a standard socket

Top notch components

Neematic integrates Pinion P1.9 XR transmission with 9 real gears for pedaling. This complements Neematic with bike-feel and prolongs your joy.

Neematic FR/1 is equiped with Fox 40 fork with 203 mm travel.

Braking is further enhanced by Hope Tech 3 V4, hard hitting downhill brakes.

Rear shock is award winning Fox with adjustable compression and rebound.
